Sunday's Sermon

Bible Reading

Arise and Shine
Isaiah 35

Sunday's Sermon
  • We should be reminded of the goodness of God.
  • Let's be reminded that this is the year that we will rise and shine.
  • In verse 3 of Isaiah 35, we have a great promise.
  • We have been given a word of hope, a word of strength.
  • God is not saying that things won't happen during the year, but He says he will give us the breakthrough.
  • God is reminding us that He will turn things to work for our own good.
  • None of us can stop events from happening.
  • Roses don't grow in the desert.
  • We are being reminded that in the amidst of what happens, God will make roses blossom.
  • Life sometimes is like a mirrage; there are false hopes here and there.
  • The bible is telling us that God will take a mirrage and make it a spring of water.
  • We are being reminded that even our craziest dreams can happen.
  • In Isaih 60, we are told to rise and shine; God is not telling us that we will be risen.
  • We are being reminded that regardless of where we are, we should rise.
